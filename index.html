Who We Are

A mission rooted in opportunity

Elevated was founded by students from Reading's most prestigious schools — Reading School and Abbey School. Having sat the 11+ ourselves, we know exactly what it takes, and we're passionate about giving every child the best opportunity, and helping other children along the way through our partnerhip with Right to Succeed.

How Elevated works

From your first enquiry to exam day, we're with you every step.

01

Free Consultation

Book a no-obligation call with one of our tutors to discuss your child's current level, target schools, and exam timeline.

02

Tailored Plan

We create a personalised study plan covering all 11+ subjects, adapted to your child's strengths and areas for growth.

03

Online Sessions

Weekly 1-to-1 or small group sessions delivered entirely online. All you need is a device and an internet connection. No travel, no fuss.
